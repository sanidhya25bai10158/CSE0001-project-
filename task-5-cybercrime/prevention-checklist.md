# Digital Safety Routine — Quick Checklist  

- Think twice before sharing personal details like your phone number, address, or ID proofs online  
- Keep your social media accounts private or limited to people you actually know  
- Always double-check the receiver’s name before sending money via UPI  
- Avoid saving card or UPI details on apps you don’t fully trust  
- Do not accept random payment requests from unknown contacts  
- Be cautious of urgent messages asking for money or login details—they’re often scams  
- Avoid downloading files or apps from unofficial or suspicious sources  
- Cross-check job offers, internships, or giveaways that seem too good to be true  
- Regularly review your bank statements and UPI transaction history  
- Change your passwords every few months, even if it feels incovenient  
- Delete old apps or accounts you no longer use  

**If something goes wrong:**  
Report immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
Or call the helpline: **1930**
